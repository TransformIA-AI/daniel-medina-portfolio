# Public-safe Boundary

This repository is a public professional portfolio.

## Allowed

- Public CV PDFs.
- Public-safe case studies.
- Public-safe descriptions of work.
- Mock/demo data.
- Deterministic recruiter concierge.
- High-level architecture and process descriptions.

## Forbidden

- Private TransformIA source code.
- Customer data.
- API keys or credentials.
- Private emails or internal documents.
- Full CS50 assessment solutions.
- Legal or litigation-sensitive material.
- Claims of guaranteed ROI.
- Claims of official AI Act certification.
- Claims of autonomous recruitment/calendar action.

> All automation must remain demo-only unless explicitly reviewed and approved.
